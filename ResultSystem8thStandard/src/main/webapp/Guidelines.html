Ledger Section

When we Click Ledger Option Then you can see Three Options Display Data , Display Marks and Display All Records


When you Click Display Data Button Your All Records will be Display . It is also same for Display Marks and Display All . Display All is Display the records in One table Student Data and Student Marks if there is no matching roll number in student data and studennt marks at that time our record will not display

Delete Section

You can Enter a Roll Number and press Enter Button in our Keyboard

Your record will be Deleted. It is for data and as well as marks

Search Section

You can Enter a Roll Number and press Enter Button in our Keyboard

Your record will be Display. It is for data and as well as marks

Insertion Section

First Insert Student data like Student Roll Number , Student Name , Student Date of Birth and Gender and Click the save button or Press Enter Button


Your data will be stored to the database and we can see this data using Search data Option or We can go to the Ledger Option and Click Display Data Button


It is same for Insert Marks

Update Section

When you need to Update the data then Click Update data and put the data and Click the Update data button or Press the Enter Button


Your data will be Updated you can see your data through Search Data or through Ledger Option and Display Data


It is same for Update Marks fill the text boxes and click the Update data or press Enter Key Your Marks will be Updated
